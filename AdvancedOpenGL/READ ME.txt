My extra feature is planet textures.
I have added 128x128 textures and mapped them to every spherical planet. Furthermore, I have added custom lighting to the worldspace, and I added toggleable planet names that float above the planets.
And in addition, I should mention that I used a Quaternion method of manipulating the camera. This gives control over the forward vector and such.
